What's the Difference Between a Laptop & Desktop?

Laptop

Portable, battery-powered, folds up for travel. Great if you want to move around the house or take your computer with you.

Desktop

Larger, stays in one place, usually has a separate monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Typically offers more power and bigger screen.

TIP: If you mostly use your computer at home and prefer a bigger screen, a desktop is probably the best choice for you. If you like to use your computer in different locations, go with a laptop!
