About SmartSass Tech

Patient, Jargon-Free Support

At SmartSass Tech, we believe technology should empower you, not frustrate you. That's why we specialize in patient, jargon-free technology support designed specifically for older adults and beginners in the Rochester, NY area.

Our Approach

We understand that technology can be overwhelming. Our personalized, one-on-one sessions are conducted at your pace, in plain English. We never rush you, and we never make you feel silly for asking questions. Every question is valid, and we're here to help you feel confident with your devices.

What We Help With

  • Devices: Computers, laptops, smartphones, tablets, printers, and smart TVs
  • Setup & Configuration: Getting your new devices ready to use
  • Troubleshooting: Fixing problems and errors
  • Training: Learning how to use your technology effectively
  • Internet & Email: WiFi setup, email configuration, and online safety
  • Video Calls: Zoom, FaceTime, and staying connected with family
  • Security: Password management, scam protection, and secure banking
  • Software Updates: Keeping your devices safe and up-to-date
  • Cloud Storage: iCloud, Google Photos, and organizing your memories

Why Choose Us?

✓ Patience First: We take the time to explain things clearly and thoroughly.
✓ No Jargon: We speak in plain English, not tech-speak.
✓ Personalized Support: One-on-one sessions tailored to your needs.
✓ Local to Rochester: Serving our community with pride.
✓ All Skill Levels Welcome: From complete beginners to those wanting to learn something new.
