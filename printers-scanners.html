Choosing the Right Printer

Inkjet Printers

Great for photos and color documents. typically more affordable upfront but ink cartridges need to be replaced regularly. Perfect for home use and occasional printing.

Laser Printers

Fast, efficient, and cost-effective for high-volume printing. Uses toner instead of ink, which lasts longer. Best for text documents and regular printing needs.

All-in-One

Combines printer, scanner, and copier in one device. Some models also include fax capability. Great space-saver and convenient for home offices.

TIP: If you print occasionally and want color photos, go with an inkjet. If you print frequently (especially black and white), a laser printer will save you money in the long run!
