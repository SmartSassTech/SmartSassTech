What's the Difference Between a Tablet & Phone?

Tablet

Larger screen (typically 7-13 inches), great for reading, watching videos, and browsing. Some tablets can make calls, but most focus on apps and media consumption.

Phone

Portable, fits in your pocket, makes calls and texts. Modern smartphones do everything: photos, videos, apps, navigation, and staying connected on the go.

TIP: If you want something easy to carry everywhere for calls and quick tasks, go with a phone. If you prefer a bigger screen for reading or watching videos at home, consider a tablet!
