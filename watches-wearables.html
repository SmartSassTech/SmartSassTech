What is a Smartwatch Used For?

Smartwatch

Wearable devices that track your health, show notifications from your phone, and help you stay connected without pulling out your phone. Great for fitness tracking, monitoring heart rate, and getting quick updates.

Other Wearables

Includes fitness bands, smart rings, and specialized trackers. These devices focus on specific health metrics like sleep quality, activity levels, or continuous health monitoring.
