Enhancing Your Tech Setup

Keyboards & Mice

Comfortable keyboards and mice make computing easier. Wireless options reduce clutter, while ergonomic designs help prevent strain. Great for improving productivity and comfort.

Monitors & Displays

Additional monitors expand your workspace. Larger screens make reading easier. Consider size (measured diagonally), resolution, and whether you need multiple monitors.

Webcams & Headsets

Essential for video calls and online meetings. Good webcams provide clear video, while quality headsets ensure crisp audio for calls without disturbing others.

TIP: Start with the basics! A comfortable keyboard and mouse make the biggest difference in daily use. Add monitors and other accessories as needed.
