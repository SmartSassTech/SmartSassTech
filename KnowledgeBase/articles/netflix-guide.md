Netflix is a streaming service for movies and TV shows. This guide covers the basics and how to fix common problems.

---

## 1. Getting Started

* **Sign Up:** Go to [netflix.com](https://www.netflix.com).
* **Plans:** Choose from Standard with Ads, Standard (no ads), or Premium (4K). [Compare Plans](https://help.netflix.com/en/node/24926)
* **Install:** Download the Netflix app on your [Smart TV, Phone, or Tablet](https://help.netflix.com/en/node/14361).

---

## 2. Managing Your Account

Most account changes must be done in a web browser, not the TV app.

* **Cancel Membership:** [Cancel here](https://www.netflix.com/cancelplan). No fees to cancel.
* **Change Plan:** [Change Plan](https://www.netflix.com/ChangePlan).
* **Update Payment:** [Manage Payment Info](https://www.netflix.com/YourAccountPayment).
* **Parental Controls:** [Set maturity ratings and PINs](https://help.netflix.com/en/node/264).

---

## 3. Troubleshooting

Having trouble watching? Try these steps first.

## Is Netflix Down?

Check the official status page: [Is Netflix Down?](https://help.netflix.com/en/is-netflix-down)

## Check Your Speed

Netflix requires a steady internet connection.

* Test your speed specifically for Netflix at: **[Fast.com](https://fast.com)** (powered by Netflix).
* **Minimum speeds:**
  * Standard (HD): 3 Mbps
  * Premium (4K): 15+ Mbps

## Fix Error Codes

If you see a code like `UI-800-3` or `NW-2-5`:

1. Go to the [Netflix Help Center](https://help.netflix.com/).
2. Type the code into the search bar.
3. Follow the specific steps for that code.

## Device Issues

If the app crashes or won't load:

1. **Restart your device** (unplug TV/console for 1 minute).
2. **Clear App Cache** (Android/Fire TV).
3. **Reinstall the App**.

## Common Questions

## How much does Netflix cost?  

Three plans: Standard with Ads ($6.99/month), Standard ($15.49/month), and Premium ($22.99/month). Cancel anytime with no cancellation fee. Prices vary by country.

## Can I watch Netflix for free?  

Netflix no longer offers free trials in most regions. Some phone carriers include Netflix with certain plans. Can use gift cards if you don't want to use credit card.

## How many people can watch at once?  

Depends on plan: Standard with Ads and Standard allow 2 simultaneous streams, Premium allows 4. This is for watching different content at same time—unlimited people can use account at different times.

## Can I download Netflix shows?  

Yes, on phones and tablets (not computers or TVs). Most content available for download. Download limits based on plan: Standard with Ads and Standard get 2 devices, Premium gets 6 devices.

## Does Netflix have commercials?  

Standard with Ads plan has brief commercials. Standard and Premium plans are completely ad-free. Ads on lower tier similar to traditional TV but less frequent.

## What shows and movies does Netflix have?  

Thousands of titles including Netflix Originals (exclusive content), licensed TV shows, movies, documentaries, stand-up specials, and more. Content varies by country. Library changes regularly as licenses expire and new content added.

## Can I share my Netflix password?  

Netflix account intended for one household (people living at same address). Can use on personal devices when traveling. Additional members may cost extra depending on plan and region.

## Why does Netflix remove shows?  

Licensing agreements expire. When Netflix's license to show content ends, must remove it. Netflix Originals stay permanently. Check "Last chance to watch" section for content leaving soon.

## Can I watch Netflix offline?  

Yes, download shows and movies to phone or tablet. Watch without internet connection. Great for flights, commutes, areas with poor connection.

## How do I get rid of something in "Continue Watching"?  

Select title, click down arrow or three dots, choose "Remove from Continue Watching." Or watch to end—shows disappear after completion. Can also hide from viewing activity in Account settings.

## What is Netflix Originals?  

Content produced or exclusively distributed by Netflix. Includes popular shows like Stranger Things, The Crown, Squid Game, Wednesday, and many others. Marked with red "N" badge.

## Can I change my plan anytime?  

Yes, upgrade or downgrade whenever you want. Changes take effect immediately (upgrades) or next billing cycle (downgrades). No penalties for changing.

## Quick Reference Guide

**Sign up:** Netflix.com → Get Started → Enter email → Create password → Choose plan → Add payment  
**Install app:** App Store or Google Play → Search "Netflix" → Download → Sign in  
**Find content:** Browse home screen rows → Click TV Shows/Movies → Use Search → Check My List  
**Watch:** Click title → Play → Use controls at bottom → Skip intro → Auto-play next episode  
**Subtitles:** While watching → Click speech bubble icon → Select language  
**Create profile:** Profile icon → Manage Profiles → Add Profile → Enter name  
**Kids profile:** Create profile → Check "Kid" box → Set age range  
**Download:** Mobile app → Find title → Tap download icon → Access in Downloads section  
**Change plan:** Account → Change plan → Select new tier → Confirm  
**Cancel:** Account → Cancel Membership → Confirm

## Need Help?

**Can't sign in:**

* Check email and password spelling
* Reset password (Forgot Password link)
* Try different email addresses you use
* Contact Netflix support

**Video won't play:**

* Check internet connection
* Restart Netflix app
* Update Netflix app
* Restart device

**Buffering issues:**

* Test internet speed (fast.com)
* Reduce video quality
* Close other apps using internet
* Restart router

**Payment problems:**

* Update payment method in Account
* Check card hasn't expired
* Verify billing address
* Contact your bank
