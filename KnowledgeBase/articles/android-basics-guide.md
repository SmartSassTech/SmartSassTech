## 2. Your Three "Main Desks"

Your phone is organized into three main areas:

1. **The Home Screen:** This is like your physical desk. Put the apps you use every day (like Phone or Camera) here.
2. **The All-Apps List:** Swipe **up** from the middle of your Home screen to see every single app on your phone. If you "lose" an app, it‚Äôs always hiding here.
3. **The Control Center:** Swipe **down** from the very top of the screen. This is where you turn on Wi-Fi, adjust the brightness, or turn on the Flashlight.

## 4. How to Get New Apps

To get new things like Facebook, Weather, or Games, you use the **Google Play Store**.

1. Look for the icon that looks like a **colorful triangle**.
2. Tap the **Search bar** at the top and type what you want (e.g., "WhatsApp").
3. Tap the green **Install** button. The app will appear in your "All-Apps List" once it's done.

## üõ†Ô∏è The "Universal Fix" for Problems

If your phone is acting slow, an app is acting "glitchy," or the screen is frozen, do not panic.

**The Magic Trick:** Restart the phone.

1. Hold down the **Power Button** (usually on the right side) for 5 seconds.
2. Tap **Restart** on the screen.
*This fixes 90% of technology problems by giving the phone a fresh start.*
