Protect your photos, contacts, and messages by backing them up automatically.

## iPhone: iCloud Backup

1. Connect your iPhone to Wi-Fi.
2. Go to **Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup**.
3. Turn on **Back Up This iPhone**.
4. Tap **Back Up Now** to start a backup right away.

## Android: Google Backup

1. Go to **Settings > System > Backup** (or **Settings > Google > Backup**).
2. Turn on **Backup by Google One**.
3. Tap **Back up now**.

## Google Photos (Both Phones)

To protect your photos and videos specifically (unlimited storage options available):

1. Open the **Google Photos** app.
2. Tap your **Profile Picture**.
3. Turn on **Backup**. This saves your photos to the cloud safely.

## Backing Up to a Computer

Use this if you don't want to use cloud storage.

* **iPhone:** Connect to a computer. Open **Finder** (Mac) or **iTunes** (Windows). Select your phone and click **Back Up Now**.
* **Android:** Connect to a computer via USB. Choose "File Transfer" on your phone screen. Open the phone's folder on your computer and copy the **DCIM** and **Pictures** folders to your desktop.
