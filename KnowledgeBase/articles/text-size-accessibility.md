If you are struggling to read the text on your screen, here is how to make it bigger on any device.

## iPhone & iPad

1. Go to **Settings > Display & Brightness > Text Size**.
2. Drag the slider to the right.
3. **Still too small?** Go to **Settings > Accessibility > Display & Text Size > Larger Text** for even larger options.

## Android (Samsung, Pixel, etc.)

1. Go to **Settings > Display > Font size and style**.
2. Drag the slider to adjust the size.

## Windows Computer

1. Go to **Start > Settings > Accessibility > Text size**.
2. Drag the slider to the right and click **Apply**.
3. **Quick Zoom:** Press the **Windows Key** and the **Plus Key (+)** together to use the Magnifier.

## Mac Computer

1. Go to **System Settings > Accessibility > Display**.
2. Click **Text size** (or select a "Larger Text" resolution in Displays).
3. **Quick Zoom:** Press **Command** and **Plus (+)** in most apps to zoom in.

## Web Browsers (Chrome, Safari, Edge)

* **Keyboard Shortcut:** Hold **Ctrl** (Windows) or **Command** (Mac) and press the **Plus (+)** key to zoom in. Press **Minus (-)** to zoom out.
